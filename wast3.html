Our Events :

Paper Presentation

Guidelines:

•

Participants must submit a technical paper related to the theme of the fest.

•

Each team/individual will have 8-10 minutes for presentation, followed by 2-5 minutes Q&A.

•

The presentation must be in PPT/PDF format (Google Slides or Microsoft PowerPoint).

Poster Presentation

Guidelines:

•

Participants will get 5 minutes to explain their poster.

•

Judges may ask questions for 2 minutes.

•

Posters must have: Title, Abstract (Brief summary of research/idea), Graphics & Diagrams (High-quality, relevant), Conclusion.

Tech Quiz

Guidelines:

•

Teams of 1-2 participants.

•

Don't involve while others are participating.

•

If you know the answer, raise your hand or write it on paper.

•

For giving the answers, you have 1 minute.

Speed Typing

Guidelines:

•

Individual participation only.

•

You have to complete the task in given time, with less mistakes.

•

We are providing a hard copy of the topic.

•

Participants must attend at the mentioned time.

Website Development Competition

Guidelines:

•

Individual or team (max 3 members).

•

Participants must design and develop a website based on a given theme.

•

Allowed Technologies: HTML, CSS.

•

Participants must submit their source code and a working demo.
